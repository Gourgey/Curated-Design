CURATED DESIGN

How the studio works

Every interior begins with how it will be used day to day: the routines, the circulation, and the way light changes across a space. From there, we build a material language that feels calm, tactile, and quietly confident.

What defines the work

Clean lines, warm proportions, and details that hold up at close range — joinery, hardware, finishes, and the way materials meet.

Rather than chasing trends, the focus is on layouts that genuinely work, robust materials, and a palette that can evolve with your life or brand.

Focus areas

  • Residential homes and apartments
  • Hospitality: restaurants, bars, boutique stays
  • Interior architecture and spatial planning
  • Furniture, lighting, and styling

Process

01. Brief & concept

We define needs, constraints, and priorities, then develop the initial concept with layouts and mood direction.

02. Design development

We refine plans, finishes, lighting, and joinery detail, aligning the design intent with budget realities.

03. Delivery & styling

We coordinate with contractors/suppliers as needed, then complete the space with furniture, art, and styling.

Locations & scope

Based in London, working across the UK and selected international projects. Collaborations include architects, developers, and private clients on both new-build and refurbishment schemes.
