CURATED DESIGN

A clear, considered direction for your space—defining layout, mood, material palette, and key pieces—before you commit to construction, joinery, or procurement.

At a glance

  • Timeline: typically 1–2 weeks
  • Includes: layout options and mood/material direction
  • Revisions: one refinement round
  • Deliverable: a concise, action-ready concept PDF

What you receive

  • Initial discovery call and brief refinement
  • A clear concept narrative defining the design intent
  • Mood, colour, and material direction
  • One to two spatial layout options
  • Key furniture and lighting direction
  • A concise concept document ready to act on

Process

  1. Brief alignment and reference review
  2. Concept development and layout options
  3. One refinement round
  4. Final concept pack issued

Additional revisions or spaces can be scoped clearly if required.

Ideal for

  • Single-room projects and focused refreshes
  • Early-stage renovations before builder pricing
  • Hospitality or commercial concepts needing clarity
  • Clients who want confidence before significant spend
