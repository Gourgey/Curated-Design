Overview

Shoreditch Office explores a softer, more residential approach to workplace design — balancing focus, hospitality, and comfort without turning into “airport lounge office”.

The intent is to create clarity through zoning and circulation, then reinforce it with a restrained palette, tactile materials, and lighting that behaves well across the whole day.

Design intent

Make the space feel composed and confident — where the architecture does the heavy lifting, and the furniture and lighting support how people actually work.
