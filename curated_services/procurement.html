CURATED DESIGN

End-to-end coordination of ordering, tracking, and delivery—so the right items arrive at the right time, without you having to manage suppliers, spreadsheets, or follow-ups.

At a glance

  • Timeline: runs alongside furnishing / build programmes
  • Includes: ordering, tracking, supplier coordination, issue resolution
  • Revisions: ongoing adjustments as required
  • Deliverable: fully managed procurement process with clear records

What you receive

  • Ordering placed with approved suppliers on your behalf
  • Lead-time tracking and delivery coordination
  • Liaison with suppliers, makers, and couriers
  • Issue handling (damages, delays, substitutions)
  • Storage coordination where required
  • A clear procurement log so everything is accounted for

Process

  1. Items confirmed and signed off for ordering
  2. Orders placed and lead times verified
  3. Ongoing tracking and supplier communication
  4. Delivery coordination with site or property access
  5. Resolution of any issues prior to installation

Procurement can run quietly in the background or be closely coordinated with contractors and installers, depending on the project.

Ideal for

  • Projects with multiple suppliers and long lead times
  • Clients who don’t want to manage ordering admin
  • Renovations where timing is critical
  • Remote or overseas clients
  • Hospitality or retail projects with tight opening dates
