CURATED DESIGN

Architectural detailing that elevates the space—joinery, mouldings, finishes, and fixed elements—designed with proportion, function, and buildability in mind.

At a glance

  • Timeline: typically 2–4 weeks (scope-dependent)
  • Includes: detail direction and key fixed-element design
  • Revisions: one refinement round
  • Deliverable: a clear, build-ready design pack (scope-based)

What you receive

  • Discovery call and measured brief alignment
  • Architectural concept for fixed elements (where relevant)
  • Joinery / millwork direction and functional planning
  • Material and finish specification for key surfaces
  • Lighting and architectural feature direction
  • A scoped pack suitable for builder / joiner pricing

Process

  1. Brief alignment + reference / precedent review
  2. Design development (fixed elements + detailing)
  3. One refinement round and coordination notes
  4. Final design pack issued for pricing / build

Drawings and technical depth are tailored to your scope—simple refreshes through to renovation-level detail.

Ideal for

  • Renovations involving joinery, wall treatments, or built-ins
  • Spaces that need architectural cohesion—proportion and rhythm
  • Clients wanting clarity before engaging trades
  • Hospitality and retail requiring repeatable, robust detailing
