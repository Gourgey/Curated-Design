CURATED DESIGN

The finishing layer that makes a space feel lived-in, intentional, and complete—art, objects, textiles, and final placement curated with restraint and rhythm.

At a glance

  • Timeline: typically 1–3 weeks (scope-dependent)
  • Includes: styling direction, sourcing, and placement
  • Revisions: one refinement round
  • Deliverable: a curated styling schedule (scope-based)

What you receive

  • Styling concept and tone (what to add, remove, and refine)
  • Artwork direction and placement strategy
  • Textile layering: cushions, throws, rugs, and window dressing notes
  • Objects and accessories shortlist (balanced, not busy)
  • Guidance on scale, height variation, and composition
  • A clear schedule for purchasing and final placement

Process

  1. Brief and reference review (what you like, what you don’t)
  2. Shortlist and styling plan (art, textiles, objects)
  3. One refinement round to align tone and budget
  4. Final schedule and optional on-site styling session

Styling can work as a stand-alone finishing layer, or as the final step after furnishing and procurement.

Ideal for

  • Homes that feel “almost finished” but not resolved
  • New moves, refreshes, and post-renovation completion
  • Clients who want polish without clutter
  • Properties being photographed, marketed, or prepared for guests
