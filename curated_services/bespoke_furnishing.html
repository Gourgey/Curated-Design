CURATED DESIGN

Furniture and key pieces sourced (or made) to fit your space and the way you use it— proportion, comfort, and longevity first.

At a glance

  • Timeline: typically 2–6 weeks (scope-dependent)
  • Includes: sourcing, specification, and layout coordination
  • Revisions: one refinement round
  • Deliverable: a curated furnishings schedule (scope-based)

What you receive

  • Discovery call to align style, budget, and comfort requirements
  • Furniture concept direction (key categories + priorities)
  • Curated options for each item (with lead times and sizing notes)
  • Finish and fabric recommendations tailored to lifestyle and durability
  • Space planning checks for proportion and circulation
  • A clear specification schedule for ordering

Process

  1. Brief + measurements (or existing plans) reviewed
  2. Shortlist creation (options with pros / cons)
  3. One refinement round and final selections
  4. Final schedule prepared for purchasing / delivery coordination

This can be purely sourcing, or paired with bespoke pieces where dimensions or finish need to be exact.

Ideal for

  • Rooms that feel “nearly there” but lack the right anchors
  • Clients who want fewer, better options (not endless browsing)
  • Upgrades that need to fit awkward footprints or tight clearances
  • Projects where lead time and logistics must be managed calmly
