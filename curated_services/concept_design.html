Concept Design

A clear direction for your space—layout, mood, palette, and key pieces—before you commit to build, joinery, or procurement.

Typical timeline: 1–2 weeks depending on scope and feedback.

What you receive

  • • Discovery call + brief refinement
  • • Concept narrative (the “why” behind the design)
  • • Mood & material direction (palette + finishes)
  • • Space planning (1–2 layout options)
  • • Key furniture/lighting direction + hero pieces
  • • A concise concept PDF you can act on

Process

  1. 1. Brief + reference review
  2. 2. Initial concepts + layout options
  3. 3. One refinement round
  4. 4. Final concept pack delivered

If you need a second revision round or extra rooms, we’ll scope it clearly before proceeding.

Ideal for

  • • One-room refreshes (living rooms, bedrooms, entrances)
  • • Early-stage renovations (before builder quotes)
  • • Hospitality concepts needing a strong identity
  • • Clients who want clarity before spending heavily
