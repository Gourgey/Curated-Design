CURATED DESIGN

Clear, proportioned drawings that translate design intent into something builders, joiners, and fabricators can actually work from—reducing ambiguity, errors, and on-site guesswork.

At a glance

  • Timeline: typically 1–3 weeks (scope-dependent)
  • Includes: plans, elevations, key dimensions, and notes
  • Revisions: one refinement round
  • Deliverable: a coordinated drawing set suitable for pricing and build

What you receive

  • Dimensioned floor plans where required
  • Elevations for walls, joinery, and fixed elements
  • Key heights, clearances, and alignments
  • Notes clarifying intent where drawings alone aren’t enough
  • Coordination between architectural elements and furnishings
  • A drawing pack scaled and formatted for professional use

Process

  1. Review of brief, site information, and existing drawings
  2. Draft plans and elevations issued for review
  3. One refinement round to resolve adjustments
  4. Final drawings prepared for pricing or construction

The level of technical detail is tailored to your project—light-touch for simple works, deeper coordination where complexity demands it.

Ideal for

  • Renovations requiring clarity before engaging trades
  • Joinery, kitchens, and bespoke elements
  • Projects where multiple contractors need alignment
  • Clients who want fewer questions once work begins
  • Build stages where decisions need to be locked in
